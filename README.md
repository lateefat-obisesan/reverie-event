#  Reverie Event Planning

## 📌 Description

Reverie Event Planning is a thoughtfully crafted website designed to reflect the heart of our company: a team dedicated to creating meaningful, beautifully coordinated celebrations. Inspired by the idea of *reverie* — a moment of daydreaming — the site showcases our approach to transforming visions into immersive, personal experiences.

The website highlights our services, philosophy, and design style through a clean, elegant layout. Visitors can explore our story, browse the services we offer, view curated galleries and testimonials, and connect with us through a validated contact form. Every section is built to mirror the calm, intentional, and creative spirit that defines Reverie Event Planning.


## ✨ Main Features
- **Three-page website** with a consistent header, footer, and responsive layout:
  - **Header** with navigation menu and a floating login modal.
  - **Footer** with social media links and additional company information.
  - **Index page** featuring a hero banner, service listings, gallery, testimonials, about section, call‑to‑action, and a fully validated contact form.
  - **About page** describing the company’s history, mission, and approach.
  - **Services page** showcasing detailed information about the different types of services offered.
